Virtual Reality Fitness

Introduction:

Generally when person does exercise he faces lots of difficulties at first. So in this application, the user will be given virtual environment for physical fitness where the user can experience himself exercising in virtual world with exact reflection of his actions performed in real world. Basically it is interaction between real and virtual worlds. sensors plays major role for giving user a virtual world experience.
